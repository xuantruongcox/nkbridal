About us

Hello beautiful ladies who are looking for the perfect formal or wedding dress,

You have arrived at the right place! We are a very unique custom-made business which works so closely with our dear customers to create their dream dresses for their most important events such as wedding, formal, prom, birthday, debutante and so on. So if you cannot find your dream dress from our extensive in-house collections, please send us an email and state your budget or the style you like, we can make your dream a reality.

Furthermore, you can trust our excellent tailoring and designing abilities since we understand different body types and we work with all types of fabric to create the dreamiest dresses. Every dress will be checked carefully before shipping to you since your satisfaction is our utmost priority.
